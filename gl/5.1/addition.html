Notes

  • In traditional Leiden the place of added text is not specified in the transcription, but noted in the apparatus or commentary. This added information, recorded here by use of the place attribute, is therefore optional and may be omitted without harm.

Questions and issues

  • In some Leiden-styles (e.g. papyrological) the place of an insertion changes the sigla used to represent the added text. The modularized XSL reflects this to some extent, but there is not yet any way to test this in the Guidelines.
