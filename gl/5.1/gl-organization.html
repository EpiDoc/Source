These Guidelines are organized into several chapters reflecting the major procedural steps that lead to a published epigraphic edition. Each of these chapters is further subdivided so as to provide discrete sections for each distinct activity or semantic distinction made in standard epigraphic practice. An appendex, dealing with a range of technical matters, follows these thematic chapters. Its contents are referenced as necessary in the appropriate chapters.
