The EpiDoc community is collaborating to produce a standard, multilingual vocabulary to describe and classify the types of monuments and objects bearing inscribed texts. This effort is being coordinated with the direction of the EAGLE constituent databases to ensure compatibilty for EAGLE-conformant EpiDoc. For tagging guidance, see Describing the type of inscribed object

The current draft version of the full vocabulary is maintained in a GoogleDoc spreadsheet for ready access by all collaborators. The following list of keys and English terms is provided here only for example. Parties who wish to review or contribute to the full vocabulary should enquire on the Markup List.
