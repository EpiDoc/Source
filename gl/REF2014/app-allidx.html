Appendix: Vocabulary and Indexing Guidelines

Indexing terms in the text (words, names, places, keywords)

  • Type of text, general keywords
  • Words and lemmatization
  • Personal names and prosopography
  • Place names and geographical terms mentioned in the text
  • Titles, offices, political posts, honorifics, occupations
  • Military, religious, socio-economic terminology
  • Dates and month names; age at death on funerary texts

Authorities and taxonomies

  • Persons and names
  • Place authorities and gazetteers
  • Event lists
  • Other/generic authority lists
  • Authority lists not in TEI
