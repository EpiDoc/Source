Numbers With a Range of Values

  • TEI definition: num

Use <num> with atLeast and atMost values.

‘’Silvio Panciera ...Josh Sosin
<num atLeast="26" atMost="29">XXVI<gap
   reason="lost"
   extent="unknown"
   unit="character"/>
</num>
