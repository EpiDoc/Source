Structuring the Digital Title Page ('teiHeader')

The details of the information required for <teiHeader> are recorded using the <fileDesc>, <encodingDesc>, <profileDesc> and <revisionDesc> elements (and their subordinate elements), which are described in the following sections. In general, the EpiDoc guidelines follow closely the TEI guidelines for their use.
