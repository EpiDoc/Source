Traditional typographic representation

  • Panciera, pp.18-19; Dohnicht, p.4:

    Letters, inscribed in place of earlier letters that have been erased, which are plainly or reasonably readable.

    1. <<abc>>
      (the added text can be clearly read)
    2. <<ABC>>
      (the added text can be clearly read, but those characters do not make sense)
    3. <<ạḅc̣>>
      (the added characters are degraded in some way that would render their identity ambiguous if encountered outside their present context)
  • Panciera, p.19:

    Letters, inscribed in place of earlier letters that have been erased, which by virtue of damage to the surface or fracture, are wholly lost

    • <<[abc]>>
      (editor supplements lost added characters with full confidence; see: Editorial supplement for characters wholly lost)
    • <<[ · · · ]>>
      (editor cannot supplement lost added characters, but is certain of the number of missing characters; see: Letters completely lost)
    • <<[ · c.5 · ]>>
      (editor cannot supplement lost added characters, and is not fully certain of the number of missing characters, see: Letters completely lost)
    • <<[ - - - ]>>
      (editor cannot supplement lost added characters, and cannot be certain of the number of missing characters due to wide variation in the size of characters elsewhere in the inscription; see: Letters completely lost (extent less certain))
  • Not explicitly addressed elsewhere:
    • legio XIII <<g>>emina
      (text has been added over a deletion, the added text can be clearly read and the deleted text is utterly unrecoverable)
    • legio XIII <<g>>emina
      (text has been added over a deletion, the added text can be clearly read and the deleted text can also be clearly read: a note would be added in the apparatus indicating that the original character was "f")

EpiDoc Encoding

  1. <app><rdg><del status="unremarkable"><gap reason="lost" extent="3" unit="character" /></del></rdg><rdg><add place="overstrike">abc</add></rdg></app>
  2. <app><rdg><del status="unremarkable"><gap reason="lost" extent="3" unit="character" /></del></rdg><rdg><add place="overstrike"><sic n="unintelligible">ABC</sic></add></rdg></app>
  3. <app><rdg><del status="unremarkable"><gap reason="lost" extent="3" unit="character" /></del></rdg><rdg><add place="overstrike"><unclear cert="high" reason="undefined">abc</unclear></add></rdg></app>
  4. legio XIII <app> <rdg> <del status="unremarkable"> <gap reason="lost" extent="1" unit="character" /></del></rdg> <rdg><add place="overstrike">g</add></rdg></app>emina
  5. legio XIII <app> <rdg> <del status="unremarkable"><unclear reason="damage" cert="high">f</unclear></del></rdg> <rdg><add place="overstrike">g</add></rdg></app>emina
