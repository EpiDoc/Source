Images, figures and facsimile

2017-03-14

It is possible to link to digital versions of photographs, drawings, squeezes and other images or representations of the text (or other views of the support) using the <facsimile> element.

  • TEI definition: facsimile
  • TEI definition: surface
  • TEI definition: graphic
