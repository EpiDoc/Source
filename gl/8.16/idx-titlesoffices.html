Titles, offices, political posts, honorifics, occupations

Tagging of different types of offices, posts, titles etc.

  • TEI definition: w
  • TEI definition: rs

Titles, offices, posts etc. in the text could be extracted from the vocabulary. They can be tagged using the @lemma attribute of the <w>.

<w lemma="consul">
 <expan>
  <abbr>co</abbr>
  <ex>n</ex>
  <abbr>s</abbr>
  <ex>ul</ex>
 </expan>
</w>

Another way to do it could be using the <rs> element with @type="office" and a @key or a @ref pointing to an authority list.

<rs type="official" key="archon">ἀρχόντων</rs>, ALA 5.
