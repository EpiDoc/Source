Military, religious, socio-economic terminology

Military terminology, titulature, religious indicators, social/political/economic vocabulary are not currently being tagged in a specific way.

  • TEI definition: w
  • TEI definition: rs

One way of extracting this terminology is through the vocabulary, using the @lemma attribute of the <w> element.

Another way could be using the <rs> element and the different values of the @type attribute could be similar to the categories used by the EDH. A @key or a @ref attribute could point to an authoriry list.

<rs type="military">
 <expan>
  <abbr>uet</abbr>
  <ex>eranus</ex>
 </expan>
</rs>, IRT 108.
