Appendix: All Supporting Data Guidelines

Structure of an EpiDoc edition
  • See below for detailed guidance on individual elements

Physical description and history of text-bearing object

Physical description
  • Description and dimensions of monument or object
  • Description and layout of text field or campus
  • Description of letters or hands
  • Decoration or iconography
  • Dimensions (of object, campus or letters)
History and locations
  • Date of origin
  • Inventory numbers and repository
  • Locations:
    • Original location or locations
    • Place and context of finding
    • Last known location, other modern locations

Other commentary and documentary sections of the edition

  • External apparatus criticus
  • Translation
  • Commentary (line-by-line or introductory)
  • Bibliography
  • Figures or facsimile

Metadata

  • Title and publication information
  • Languages and scripts

Indexing terms in the text (words, names, places, keywords)

  • Words and lemmatization
  • Personal names and prosopography
  • Place names and geographical terms mentioned in the text
  • Titles, offices, political posts, honorifics, occupations
  • Military, religious, socio-economic terminology
  • Dates and month names; age at death on funerary texts
  • General keywords, other taxonomies
